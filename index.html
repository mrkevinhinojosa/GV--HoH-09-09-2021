There's just one month left until the Granville Home of Hope Grand Prize Drawing, and tickets are going fast!


Once again, we are offering a limited number of two-ticket bundles, which includes one free entry into a drawing to win a 2-year lease on a 2021 Lexus UX 200, generously donated by Fresno Lexus. There will only be 2,250 bundles available to purchase, and as of this week, there are fewer than 400 bundles remaining.


Each Granville Home of Hope ticket gives you a chance to win the Grand Prize, a brand new Granville Canvas 9 home at Copper River Ranch, valued at over $500,000. There are also more than two dozen amazing secondary prizes, including vacation packages, electronics, and more! And as always, every penny of every ticket purchased goes to 11 nonprofits to help them continue providing food, shelter, health care and education to the underserved in our community. Plus, when you purchase your ticket, you can designate which organization will receive 100% of the funds from your ticket, or you can choose to split the proceeds between all 11 nonprofits.
